Master Class in Partial Differential Equations and Applications

Aims and scope

Modelling with Partial Differential Equations (PDEs) is widely used in many scientific fields. It extends from the traditional domains such as the mechanics, physics and geometry, to new areas, e.g. the economy, social, and life sciences. The master class offers four courses, of 5H each, to students in the masters of mathematics and PhD-students. The main objective of the masterclass is to provide an overall picture of the modelling with PDEs and to give some research perspectives to the participants.
